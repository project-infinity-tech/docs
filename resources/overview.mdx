The Resource View is the backend section of the Scram editor. It gives you access to everything that lives outside of individual pages — your database, file storage, API integrations, user management, workflows, data types, deployments, and server logs.

When you first open the Resource View you land on the **Overview** — a visual map of your project's structure showing how the main app, components, built-in services, integrations, and deployments relate to each other.

---

## Project settings

The Overview page also contains project-level settings that apply across your entire app:

**Description** — a free-text field describing what your project does. This is used by the Scram AI to provide more relevant and accurate assistance, so it's worth keeping it up to date as your app evolves.

**Locale** — sets the default locale for your project. This is used whenever locale-sensitive formatting is applied — for example, date and number formatting — unless overridden at the component level.

**Timezone** — sets the default timezone for your project. Used whenever displaying dates and times unless a specific timezone override is applied.
