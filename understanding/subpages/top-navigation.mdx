---
title: "Top Navigation"
---

The top navigation bar runs across the top of the editor and is always visible regardless of which view you're in. It contains project-level controls, view switching, and status indicators.

---

## Controls

**Scrambot** — the system-level menu for platform-wide functions. See [Scrambot](/understanding/subpages/the-scrambot) for full details.

**App Manager** — opens a modal for managing the current app's pages and frontend configuration. See [App Manager](/understanding/subpages/app-manager) for full details.

**Resources** — a dropdown that lets you switch between the different sections of the Resource View, giving you quick access to the database, storage, users, and other backend areas without navigating through the sidebar.

**Insert Component** — opens the component picker, letting you add a new component to the current page.

**Theme** — opens the theme editor, where you can manage your project's global styles and design tokens.

**Canvas Settings** — controls for the canvas itself — zoom, grid, and other display preferences for the editing environment.

---

## Indicators

**Warning Indicator** — flags non-critical issues in your project that are worth reviewing but won't prevent your app from running.

**Error Indicator** — flags critical issues that will prevent your app from working correctly. Always resolve these before deploying.

---

## Actions

**Deployment Button** — deploys your app to Dev or publishes to Live. See [Deployments](/resources/deployments) for full details.

**Bug Report Button** — opens the bug reporting tool to send feedback directly to the Scram team.
