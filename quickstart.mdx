## Step 1 — Go to the Scram Demo

Open the link below in your browser.

[https://workspace.projectinfinity.tech/](https://workspace.projectinfinity.tech/)

---

## Step 2 — Sign in or create your account

You'll see the Scram sign-in screen. As you're a new user, click **"New User? Create an account"** at the bottom.

<img src="/images/step2.png" alt="Scram Sign In screen" />

You'll be taken to the Sign Up page. Enter your email and choose a password. Your password must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contain at least one number
- Include a special character (e.g. `!@#$%^&*`)

You'll receive a confirmation email with a code — enter it when prompted to verify your address.

---

## Step 3 — Create your first project

You're now on the **Projects Dashboard**. Click **Create Project** to get started. Scram will give your project a fun random name — usually a colour and an animal. You can rename it any time, or type your own name straight away.

<img src="/images/step3.png" alt="Projects Dashboard" />

<Info>It may take 15–20 seconds for your project to be created — just be patient and you will be taken to your new project!</Info>

---

## Step 4 — In the future

Next time you can just click on your project to open it from the projects list.

<Info>If you run into any issues, use the **Report a Bug** button in the top right corner of the screen.</Info>

---

## Step 5 — Ask Scram to build something

Type what you'd like to build into the chat box and press the **green arrow**. Answer any questions Scram asks, then review and approve the Plan it comes up with. That's it — Scram will get to work!

<img src="/images/step5a.png" alt="Scram chat input" />

<Info>Don't be alarmed if you see Errors or Warnings during the build — Scram handles these automatically.</Info>

---

## Step 6 — Preview your app

Once Scram is done, click the green **Deploy** button in the top right. Your app opens in a new tab.

<img src="/images/step6a.png" alt="Preview button" />

<Info>You might briefly see a black screen the first time — that's completely normal, nothing to worry about.</Info>

Your app is now live and testable! Remember that Scram builds things a little differently each time, so it may not look exactly like these examples.

<img src="/images/step6b.png" alt="Working app example" />

---

## Step 7 — Make changes and rebuild

While testing, ask Scram to make changes or fix any issues at any time — just type in the chat.

To see your changes in the Preview App, find the **Scrambot** at the bottom left of the preview page, click it, then click **Build** on the right side of the ScramBar.

<img src="/images/step7.png" alt="ScramBar with Rebuild button" />

---
