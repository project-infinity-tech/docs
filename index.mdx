## What is Scram?

Scram is a **full-stack app builder**. You describe what you want to build — in plain language, to an AI — and Scram builds it: the pages, the database, the logic, the design, all of it. The result is a real, production-quality web application, not a prototype or a mockup.

Unlike no-code tools that give you templates to fill in, Scram builds from your description. Unlike hiring a developer, it builds in seconds. And unlike most AI tools, what it produces is not throwaway code — it is a structured, maintainable application you can continue to evolve.
